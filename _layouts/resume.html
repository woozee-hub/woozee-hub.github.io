Conference Publications

[IJCAI'25] BankTweak: Adversarial Attack against Multi-Object Trackers by Manipulating Feature Banks

W. Shin, D. Kang, D. Choi, B. B. Kang, J. Lee and H. Baek

Proposed BankTweak, a novel adversarial attack on multi-object tracking (MOT) that manipulates feature banks rather than object positions, overcoming the limitations of prior methods that are easily neutralized by Mahalanobis-threshold tuning and suffer from low stealth due to increased false positives and false negatives.
